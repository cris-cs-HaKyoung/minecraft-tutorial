How to play?

When we play Minecraft we need to know what key we need to press to ,move, eat, run, etc.

  • w = ↑
  • s = ↓
  • a = ←
  • d = →
You can press ctrl(control) to run and swim.

Mining

When we mine in Minecraft we need pickaxe to mine. You will propbably need crafting table to make all kind of pickaxe and others too. There are 6 kinds of Pickaxe:

  • Wooden Pickaxe
  • Stone Pickaxe
  • Iron Pickaxe
  • Golden Pickaxe
  • Diamond Pickaxe
  • Netherite Pickaxe

You always start mining with woonden pickaxe. The list of ore you can mine with wooden pickaxe is:

  • Stone
  • Coal
  • Deepslate

The list of ore you can mine with stone pickaxe is:

  • Stone
  • Deepslate
  • Coal
  • Iron
  • Lapis Lazuli

The list of ore you can mine with iron pickaxe is:

  • Stone
  • Deepslate
  • Coal
  • Iron
  • Lapis Lazuli
  • Redstone
  • Gold
  • Diamond
  • Emerald

The list of ore you can mine with gold pickaxe is:

  • Stone
  • Deepstone
  • Coal

The list of ore you can mine with diamond pickaxe is:

  • Stone
