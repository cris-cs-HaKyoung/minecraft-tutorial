How to play?

When we play Minecraft we need to know what key we need to press to ,move, eat, run, etc. So, we have to press 'w' to walk forward, 's' to walk backward, 'a' to walk to the left side, and 'd' to walk right side. You can press ctrl(control) to run. Also, if you want to swim you can press ctrl(control).

Mining

When we mine in Minecraft we need pickaxe to mine. You will propbably need crafting table to make all kind of pickaxe and others too. There are 6 kinds of Pickaxe:

  • Wooden Pickaxe
  • Stone Pickaxe
  • Iron Pickaxe
  • Golden Pickaxe
  • Diamond Pickaxe
  • Netherite Pickaxe

You always start mining with woonden pickaxe. The list of you can mine with wooden pickaxe is:

  • Soil
  • Wood
  • Stone
  • Coal
  • Gravel
  • Deepslate
  • Sand
